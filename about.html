Bridge Core Consulting Corp was founded on a simple belief: the best IT consulting comes from people who have actually done the work and who care about outcomes, not just deliverables.

Our Mission

Bridging Business and Technology

Bridge Core Consulting Corp is a specialist IT consultancy serving enterprise clients in the UK, US and Canada. We operate across five core practice areas: Quality Assurance, Project Management, Product Management, Business Analysis, and Agile and Scrum.

Our consultants are experienced practitioners, not generalists, who embed within your team and drive measurable, lasting change. We have delivered complex programmes for some of the largest organisations in Telecom, Insurance, Financial Services, Media and E-commerce.

We do not just bridge the gap between business and IT. We close it permanently.

What sets us apart is our ability to operate across the full delivery lifecycle, from early requirements through to production release, and our deep familiarity with the enterprise tools, methodologies and operating models used in UK, US and Canadian markets.
