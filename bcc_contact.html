Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you work remotely or on site?
Both. Our consultants are based in Canada (Ontario) and have delivered remotely for clients in the UK, US and Canada. On-site engagements are available depending on location and requirements.
What size organisations do you work with?
Primarily mid-market to enterprise organisations running complex IT programmes across multiple teams or geographies. We've worked with major Telcos, Insurance firms, Media companies and SaaS businesses.
How quickly can you start an engagement?
For advisory and fixed scope engagements, we can typically start within two weeks. For embedded consulting roles, allow three to four weeks from agreement to kick-off.
Can you provide multiple consultants?
Yes. Bridge Core has a network of experienced IT professionals. For programmes requiring multiple disciplines simultaneously e.g. BA plus QA plus Scrum Master we can assemble a team.
Do you work inside SAFe environments?
Absolutely. Our senior consultants are SAFe 6 certified and have led PI Planning, managed ARTs, and operated as Lead Product Owners within large SAFe programmes at Bell Canada and CDW.
What sectors do you specialise in?
Telecom, Insurance, E-commerce, Legal Tech, Media and SaaS based on real project delivery experience. We also work across Retail, Financial Services and Public Sector.
